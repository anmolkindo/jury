ABOUT US

This webiste was created by Anmol Kindo as a college project which turned out to be a good idea as a startup project. We as professional photographers help out Enthusiasts blend among the community and help in seeing the world in others perspective. Images might be a click, on a blink of an eye but for sure has an untold tale to tell.

We in SnapLens give a platform to the photographers, both beginners and professionals to come up to the platform and make a name for themselves.



Our resourceful CEO and we his teammates are determined to provide you with the best of photographs you can find over the internet. We are more focused to deliver a quality content rather than a dissolved one.
